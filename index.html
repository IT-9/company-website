Assistant Technical Officer

Full-Time • Entry Level • Field-Based
East Midlands, UK (with travel to customer sites)
£12.50/hour (rising to £13.50 after 3-month probation)
Full-time, 37.5 hours per week

We're looking for a practical, reliable, and enthusiastic Assistant Technical Officer to join our technical team. You'll support the installation of our hardware onto buses, and occasionally assist with light assembly and preparation work at our premises.

No prior experience is required — we offer full training, clear procedures, and day-to-day support. This is a great opportunity for someone who enjoys working with their hands and wants to grow their skills in a friendly, flexible start-up environment.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist with hardware installation on buses (wiring, brackets, mounting devices)
  • Support light assembly and preparation of components in our workshop
  • Follow detailed installation and safety procedures
  • Maintain accuracy and high standards of occupational health and safety
  • Travel to customer sites as part of a team (occasional overnight stays)

What We're Looking For

  • Good level of spoken and written English
  • Outgoing, proactive attitude — comfortable working on-site with others
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to follow instructions
  • Interest in electronics, mechanical work, or transport systems
  • Willingness to travel and work occasional overtime

What We Offer

  • Full on-the-job training and clear procedures
  • Friendly, supportive team environment
  • Opportunities to progress into product development or software/hardware engineering
  • Help with accommodation for candidates relocating
  • Open to applicants on short-term working visas, including working holiday schemes
